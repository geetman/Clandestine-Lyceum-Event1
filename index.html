Why It Matters

Research at your age
is a superpower.

01

Stands Out on Applications

A published research paper in Class 8–12 is extraordinary. It sets you apart from thousands of applicants to top colleges—globally and in India and abroad, in a way no grade or award can.

02

Builds Real Thinking Skills

Research teaches you how to ask hard questions, dig into evidence, and form original conclusions. These critical thinking skills compound forever—in every subject, every career.

03

Opens Academic Doors

ORCID IDs, ResearchGate profiles, and academic citations create a traceable scholarly identity. Starting early means you build a long, impressive track record before you're 18.

04

Proves You Can Do the Hard Thing

Anyone can say they're curious. A research paper proves it. It's tangible proof of intellectual initiative that speaks louder than any exam score ever could.

"Research is not for PhDs. It is for anyone willing to ask a question that no one has answered yet."

Paid by You

Journal Publishing Fee (IJFMR)

When your paper is complete and ready to publish, IJFMR charges a standard article processing fee. This is a normal part of open-access academic publishing worldwide—and it is paid directly to the journal, not to us.

We will walk you through the exact cost at the meeting so there are no surprises. You only pay this after your paper is written and ready to submit.

Exact fee details can be found in (https://www.ijfmr.com/fees-and-payment.php).

Where Your Paper Gets Published

Your paper will be published in IJFMR — a globally recognised academic journal.

The International Journal For Multidisciplinary Research (IJFMR) is an open-access, peer-reviewed journal accepted worldwide. It welcomes high-quality research across all disciplines, making it a perfect home for your first paper — regardless of your topic.

What happens at the meeting.

Six structured modules. You walk in a student. You walk out with a research plan, academic accounts, and everything you need to get started.

01

Why Research Papers Matter

A frank conversation on what academic research actually means, why publishing at your age matters, and how a single paper can change the trajectory of your academic career.

02

Setting Up Your Academic Identity

Hands-on setup of ORCID, ResearchGate, Google Scholar, and other essential academic accounts. You'll leave with a live academic profile that grows with every paper you publish.

03

How to Actually Do Research

Where to find credible sources — Google Scholar, PubMed, Semantic Scholar, JSTOR. How to read a paper. How to know if a source is trustworthy. How to build a research database.

04

Mastering Citations

APA, MLA, IEEE — what they are and when to use them. How to use Zotero or citation tools to manage references automatically. Why citations make or break academic credibility.

05

Your Personalized Research Plan

Every participant receives a specific, actionable research plan tailored to their chosen topic and difficulty level. Not a template — a plan written for your paper.

06

Getting Started — Right Now

The exact first steps to take after the meeting. How to structure your paper. How to write an abstract. How to submit to IJFMR. The full path from idea to published author.
